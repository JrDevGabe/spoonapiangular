Bahn Mi Sandwhich

Bahn Mi Recipe – Vietnamese Sandwiches with Golden Pork Belly – Bahn Mi is the Vietnamese word for bread. In Vietnamese cuisine, it also refers to a type of short baguette with thin, crisp crust and soft...

Difficulty

Medium

Prep Time

2 Hours

Servings

2

Preparation

  • 1

    Mix garlic, hot pepper sauce and mayonnaise in a small pot, cover and chill.

  • 2

    Thinly slice an onion, jappaleno, cabbage and pickles. Toss with a pickle juice to combine; cover and chill

  • 3

    Whisk flour, pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon salt in a shallow bowl. Pour buttermilk into another shallow bowl

  • 4

    Dredge chicken in flour, shaking off excess. Dip in buttermilk allowing excess to dripback into bowl

  • 5

    Pour oil into a large heavy skillet to a depth of 1/2 inch, Heat over medium heat until the themometer registers 350 °

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken tights
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • Tomato Paste
  • 2 teaspoon + 2 tablespoon of kosher salt.
  • 2 teaspoon freshly cracked black peppeer.
  • 2 teaspoon garlic.
